## 🩸 First Blood — +50 pts per task

The first team to complete any task earns an automatic +50 First Blood bonus for that task. Each task has one First Blood available. Speed matters.

---

## 🏆 Innovation & Presentation

Innovation & Use Case and Demo & Presentation will be scored by the facilitators at the end of the event. Focus on building something meaningful and being ready to present it clearly.
